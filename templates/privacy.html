Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the Service, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you directly provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate the Service — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and provide filtering. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the features you use.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all your data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces for the interface. This involves a request to Google's servers, which may log your IP address per Google's own privacy policy.

Beyond fonts, we do not integrate analytics, advertising networks, or other third-party services that would access your data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at the contact information provided in the app.
